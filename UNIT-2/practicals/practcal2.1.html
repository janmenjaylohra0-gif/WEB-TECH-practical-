The coconut is a tropical fruit that grows on coconut palm trees. It is known for its versatility, providing food, drink, oil, and even materials for shelter.

Fun fact: Coconut water is naturally sterile and was used as an emergency IV fluid during World War II!

Coconuts are rich in fiber, vitamins, and minerals. They play an important role in many cultures, especially in South Asia and the Pacific Islands.
