About Coconut

Coconuts are tropical fruits known for their versatility. They provide food, drink, oil, and materials.

Health Benefits

Coconuts are rich in fiber, vitamins, and minerals. Coconut water is hydrating and refreshing.

Fun Fact

Coconut water was used as emergency IV fluid during World War II due to its sterility.
